Welcome to Zheen Heart Department

Our commitment is to deliver world-class cardiac care. At Zheen Heart Department, we blend state-of-the-art technology with compassionate care to ensure the best outcomes for our patients. Our team of fellowship-trained surgeons and cardiologists works around the clock to provide reliable, trustworthy treatment – from routine checkups to complex heart surgeries.

Heart Team Discussion

The era of the solitary decision-maker is over. For your patients with Left Main disease, complex multivessel CAD, or high-risk valvular pathology, we invite you to the table.

The ZHD Team is an open forum. When you refer a complex case, we don’t just treat it; we discuss it. We invite referring colleagues to join our deliberations whether in person or virtually to review the angiograms and echo data alongside our surgeons and interventionalists.

Cardiac Imaging + Complex Intervention

We have integrated a complete multi-modality imaging suite spanning advanced echocardiography TEE 3D/4D, CT, and CMR directly with cath lab capabilities. We provide the "structural intelligence" you need to make confident decisions for your most complex patients.

Advanced Cardiac ICU

We offer a fully integrated mechanical and pharmacological safety net for your complex ischemic and heart failure cases:

  • Mechanical Circulatory Support: Ready capability for IABP and ECMO (V-A/V-V) cannulation and management.
  • Advanced Renal Support: Bedside CRRT for cardiorenal syndrome and fluid overload refractory to diuretics.
  • 24/7 Airway & Hemodynamic Management: Immediate intensivist-level response for respiratory failure or shock.

Minimally Invasive Cardiac Surgery

This modern approach results in significantly less pain, a lower risk of infection, shorter hospital stays, a faster recovery, and minimal scarring compared to traditional surgery.

Advanced Mitral & Aortic Valve

REPAIR (Not Replacement)

Our philosophy is to preserve your natural heart whenever possible. While many centers immediately opt to replace a damaged heart valve with an artificial one, our surgeons possess the high-level skills necessary to repair the patient’s own valve.

Successful valve repair often means better long-term heart function and, crucially, may eliminate the need for the patient to take lifelong blood-thinning medication.

ECMO | LVAD | RVAD | Impella

ECMO is a sophisticated, life-saving technology used for patients with severe, critical heart or lung failure. It acts as an external artificial heart and lung, providing oxygen to the body when the patient's own organs are too weak to function.

Foreign Team

We have invested heavily in cutting-edge technology and specialized training to establish a cardiac center unique in the region. We handle the most complex, high-risk cases by utilizing equipment and surgical techniques that are largely unavailable elsewhere in Iraq.
